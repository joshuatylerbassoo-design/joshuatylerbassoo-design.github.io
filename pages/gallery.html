Exhibit III

The Pursuits

What I do with my hands and my time, when no one is grading the outcome.

No. 01

Rock Climbing

Where patience and grip strength matter equally. There is no shortcut up a wall — only the next hold.

No. 02

Camping & Hiking

Distance has a way of clarifying priorities. I go to the trail to think clearly and return with less noise in my head.

No. 03

Fitness

The body is the first piece of equipment. I train it daily, the same way I train every other discipline — without skipping days.

No. 04

Firearms & Marksmanship

A discipline built on respect, responsibility, and precision — values I believe extend well beyond the range.

No. 05

Service

From food drives in Queens to two years spent serving abroad, service is less a hobby than a habit I can't put down.

No. 06

Family & Tradition

I'm drawn to the model of a man who shows up quietly and consistently — providing, protecting, and present.

Beyond This Gallery

There is a quieter, rougher side to these interests —

one that doesn't belong in a formal gallery, but exists all the same. It isn't linked here. If you happen to find it, you'll know.
